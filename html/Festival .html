Festival Celebration Ideas

Explore how people celebrate festivals across the world!

Diwali

Known as the festival of lights, Diwali is celebrated with diyas, sweets, and family gatherings.

Holi

The festival of colors is celebrated by throwing colored powders and enjoying festive sweets like gujiya.

Christmas

Celebrated with decorated Christmas trees, gifts, and family dinners.
