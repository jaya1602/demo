Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Spices: turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala

Steps:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions till golden brown.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices, cook till oil separates.
  3. Add cream and paneer cubes, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • Spices: bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom
  • 1 onion (sliced)

Steps:

  1. Boil rice with spices till 70% cooked.
  2. Sauté vegetables with onions and biryani masala.
  3. Layer rice and vegetables, cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with raita.

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Spices: salt, red chili powder, cumin powder
  • Butter or ghee for cooking

Steps:

  1. Prepare dough by mixing flour with water.
  2. Mix spices with mashed potatoes for stuffing.
  3. Roll dough, add stuffing, and seal it.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa with butter/ghee.
